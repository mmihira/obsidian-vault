# Armor System

Armors are a **consumable** quasi-physical/magic item which provide protection against death.

## Mechanics

- Armors must be **applied** to the player to have an effect
- Applying armors takes seconds and is **interruptible**
- Once armors are applied they **cannot be regenerated** — a new armor must be applied

## Construction Ingredients

- [[Sanctuary/Economy/Items & Resources#WardSteel|WardSteel]]
- Crystal filament (of different levels)
- Crystal gems of some variety
- `[Optionally a modifier]` catalyst

## Cloud Resist Armour

- Special armour variants grant passive cloud resistance
- See [[Sanctuary/Combat/Fog System|Fog System]]
