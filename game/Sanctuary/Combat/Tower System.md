# Tower System

## Power Relay

- To route power around, relays must be built to expand the reach of the power network
- See also [[Sanctuary/Crystals/Crystal Power & Networks|Crystal Power & Networks]]

## Tower Modes

| Mode | Power Cost | Effect | Use Case |
| ---- | ---------- | ------ | -------- |
| **Destroy** | High | Permanently eliminates clouds in range | Establishing safe corridors |
| **Push** | Low | Repels clouds without destroying | Temporary exploration, resource conservation |

## Anti-Fog Tower

- Uses power to eliminate [[Sanctuary/Combat/Fog System|fog clouds]]
- Has a relatively short range so the player has to move it to progress through the fog
- Prevents new fog spawns in the area

## Defensive Towers

- Powered by magic crystals the player has cultivated and grown
- Players learn to position towers strategically to defend against waves
- First major introduction to tower defense gameplay happens at the [[Sanctuary/Map/Crystal Wall|Crystal Wall]]
- Towers are upgradeable to increase efficiency:
  - **Range** — larger coverage area
  - **Power efficiency** — reduced energy consumption
  - **Destruction rate** — faster cloud elimination
  - **Durability** — resistance to angry cloud attacks
  - **Auto-targeting** — prioritises threatening clouds
- Towers consume crystal resources as a trade-off

### Tower Placement Strategy

- Towers placed near network edges: safe, low yield
- Towers placed near center: high yield, triggers **angry cloud response**
- Players must balance aggression with defensive coverage

## Tower Defense at River Bridge

- The [[Sanctuary/Map/Impassable River|bridge]] can be upgraded to support defensive towers
- Enemies will attack the bridge, attempt to destroy towers, and gain control
- When enemies gain control they build their own towers and fortify
