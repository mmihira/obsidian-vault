# Survival

## Core Survival Mechanics

- **Hunger** — manage hunger by eating 2-3 times daily
- **Mana** — manage mana through crystal-crafted potions
- **Day/Night** — navigate [[Sanctuary/World/Day Night Cycle|day/night cycle]] (dark crystals spawn stronger enemies at night)
- **Sleep** — sleep mechanic with penalties/bonuses based on hunger and rest duration
