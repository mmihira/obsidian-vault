# Crystal Overview

Crystals are the central resource and mechanic of SANCTUARY. They serve as power sources, currency, crafting ingredients, and the primary driver of both progression and danger.

## Wild Crystal Lifecycle

- Crystal catalyst spreads across the map
- The fractal nature of crystal networks extends in hexagonal patterns (top-down hexagonal grid)
- Crystal networks' main goal is to continuously increase power production
- Crystal catalyst will spawn crystals (which the player can harvest)
- Crystal catalyst and crystals absorb the energy of the sun by day and expend this to grow their network

## Crystal Filament

- Used as a binding/threading agent
- Analogous to bushes — crystal structure with some crystals poking out
- Crystal networks use bushes to protect the catalyst from damage
  - Stepping on them damages the player
  - Earlier on they simply impede progress
  - Creates another natural barrier to map progress

## Consciousness (Undefined Lore)

Consciousness exists in phases:
- That which binds to complex structure to express itself and expand
- That which exists in raw form and expresses itself through use
- The third form is anti-conscious — seeks self-destruction
- Regions of the multiverse are dominated by these three phases
