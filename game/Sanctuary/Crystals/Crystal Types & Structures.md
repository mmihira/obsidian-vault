# Crystal Types & Structures

## Crystal Tiers

- `[Blue, Green, Red, Gold]` — Normal crystal levels
- **Teleporter enabling crystal** — takes time to grow, hard to get shards
- **Black crystal** — grows from death of humans; tech factions want to build CPUs, creating conscious machines

## Natural Crystal Structures

Crystals in nature appear in tiers:

### Power Crystals

- Can be tapped to generate power and connected to the player power network
- Source of [[Sanctuary/Crystals/Crystal Power & Networks|crystal networks]]
- Crystal networks expand naturally with lower-tier crystals which can be harvested
- Monsters spawn in higher numbers around power crystals and defend them
- Strategically generated on the map to incentivise exploration
- Provide a natural progression system — player must venture further for more power
- **Tiers**: Blue, Green, Red, Gold
- Each crystal can level up with enough maintenance/time

### Raw Crystals

- The network's way of storing crystal power generated from trees, bushes, and the network itself
- Non-power-generating but can be harvested by the player for raw gems
- The type predominantly farmed by the player in their crystal farm

### Dark Crystals

- Responsible for spawning powerful enemies at **night time** to constrain the player
- See also [[Sanctuary/World/Day Night Cycle|Day Night Cycle]]

### Crystal Catalyst

- Produced by crystal networks; can be harvested by the player
- Appears on the map as a liquid-like resource
- Harvesting involves pointing a spell at the area — catalyst is siphoned into inventory
- Required to feed player-grown crystals daily
- Represented as connected hexagonal areas
- Need pixel art for each possible hexagonal arrangement
- Hexagons shrink correlated with the amount of health they have
