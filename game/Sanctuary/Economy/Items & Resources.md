# Items & Resources

## Raw Gem

- Most basic ingredient
- Dropped from:
  - Destroying crystals
  - Destroying trees (drops less than crystals)
  - Monsters (sometimes)

## GemWood

- Trees grow as normal
- They show some effects of the crystal corruption

## GemPlanks

- Refined from GemWood
- Used to make [[Sanctuary/Town/Buildings|buildings]]

## WardSteel

- The derived version of metal from Earth in this dimension — infused with magic
- Required for building in order to protect
- Occurs in raw form next to crystals which emanate power
- Usually guarded by monsters (attracted/spawned by [[Sanctuary/Crystals/Crystal Types & Structures#Power Crystals|power crystals]])
- Normal rocks already on the map may transform into WardSteel
- WardSteel tech level influenced by the power crystal type

## Magic Essence

- Can be harvested from power crystals
- Monsters drop this (initial source)

## Condensed Gem Sheet

- Sheets of gem infused material used for various construction
- Made from magic essence
