#### Life cycle


Hast emergence from magic field footprints are deterministic. I.e Will it appear at 0, then at t1, appears at +2, then at t2, appears at +3


Hast start at source

- on timer, check random adjacent  slot
- if available, and no collission, place new hast
- each hast, on timer can reach next lvl (constrained by source signal)
- hast may evolve to crystal (constrained by source signal)
