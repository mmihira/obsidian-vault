# Impassable River

## Core Concept

The river is a **dynamic obstacle** that introduces crystal mechanics, strategic decision-making, and foreshadows later defensive gameplay.

## 1. Main Design

- The river is too large to cross — the player must build a **bridge**
- To cross, the player must build **three bridges**, spanning two island waypoints
- Each island waypoint contains a **milestone enemy fight** as well as some valuable treasure

## 2. Boat

- The player can build a boat to travel **along** the river
- However, crossing the river by boat is not possible — further areas have magic tides which turn the player away
- The **last island** in the chain has the switch to turn off the river tides

## 3. Strategic Crossing Points

- Several locations where the player may build bridges
- 2–3 island chains available as crossing options

## 4. Integration with Town Safety

- The river initially acts as a **natural barrier** protecting the town from the [[Sanctuary/Map/Crystal Wall|crystal wall]]'s monsters
- Once bridged, it becomes a **vulnerability** — monsters can use it to attack
- Creates tension: crossing the river advances progress but increases danger
- Foreshadows the concept that the town is never truly safe

## 5. Tower Defense

- The bridge becomes a central **tower defense mini-game**
- Bridge can be upgraded to support [[Sanctuary/Combat/Tower System|defensive towers]]
- Enemies will attack the bridge, attempt to destroy towers, and gain control
- When they gain control, they build their own towers and fortify the bridge

## 6. Optional Boss

- There is a **sea monster** in the river which the player may optionally fight
- Requires great preparation and solving a puzzle to lure the monster to the correct island
