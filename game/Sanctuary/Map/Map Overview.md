# Map Overview

## Geography

### Predominant Geometry

- The map is **open world**, but the primary direction of progression is toward the **north**
- The southern edge is blocked; sides are bordered by natural obstacles (oceans and mountains)
- Benefits:
  - Simplifies navigation and introduces natural progression in exploration
  - Easier to procedurally generate a world with a defined predominant direction
  - Simpler to design natural progression barriers placed further north from the start

## Constraints

Constraining player movement is required — the player needs obstacles to defeat:

- Enemy difficulty scales with distance from town center
- Fissures or other natural barriers which the player must overcome
- Player can't spend all time in the wild because they will run out of [[Sanctuary/Progression/Food System|food]] (and food is scarce)
- Can restrict crystal production infrastructure to also limit exploration capability

### Crystal Network Constraint

- See [[Sanctuary/Crystals/Crystal Power & Networks|Crystal Power & Networks]]

### Cliffs

- Basic cliffs should be procedurally generated for map variety
