# Mountain Tunnel

## Core Design

- Player encounters a large mountain blocking progress
- Player must build a **mini town / excavation camp** to excavate the mountain

## Excavation

- The excavation takes far too long for the player alone
- Can **hire people** from the town to do it — however must pay them and supply food
- Must manage food supply transport to the mining site

## Challenges

- Tunnel is **dark** — player must build lighting through the tunnel
- Monsters also periodically attack — need to hire protection
- Tunnel exposes **branches** with additional content

## Rewards

- Digging will uncover **important loot** potentially
- Provides incentive for the player to be involved directly
