## Crystal Power

- Magic essence / crystal power can be automatically harvested from crystals
- To convey the power, must build **relays** at periodic distances
- Can build and harvest far away resources with a good relay network
- Can collect magic essence in large storage vats (nice animation to show fluid nature of magic essence)
- Crystals generate power which powers your buildings
- Only certain spots in the game allow you to grow crystals which provide continuous power
- Otherwise generic crystals grow to a point then become static
