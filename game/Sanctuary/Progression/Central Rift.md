# The Central Rift (Long-Horizon Goal)

## Overview

A single **mega-dungeon** located on the map that represents the ultimate challenge and long-horizon endgame goal.

## Access

- Requires **keys crafted from specific crystals/cores** to descend
- Keys are assembled from materials found in [[Sanctuary/Progression/Core Depths|Core Depths]] and high-tier fog networks
- Each descent requires a new key, encouraging sustained resource gathering

## Contents

- Deepest lore and story revelations
- Most powerful rewards in the game
- Potential "ending" or **prestige reset**

## Alternate Endgame

The Central Rift represents one of two possible endgame paths:

1. **The Rift** — descend the mega-dungeon for the ultimate challenge
2. **Invasion Origin** — after repelling enough [[Sanctuary/Combat/Combat & Defense#Invasion / Assault System|assault waves]], locate and destroy the enemy origin point to end the invasion threat
