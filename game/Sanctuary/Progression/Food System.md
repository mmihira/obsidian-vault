# Food System

## Core Mechanic

- The player needs to eat at least **two/three times a day** to keep their food levels high
- If they exceed their hunger limits they get penalised:
  - Movement speed reduction
  - Increased mana consumption
  - Progressing until basically unplayable

## Scarcity

- Food is a **very scarce** asset in Sanctuary
- Human edible food does not grow in this dimension
- Food arrives via the [[Sanctuary/Economy/Export Import System|exporter]] every morning — enough at first for just one day's worth
- Food is expensive to import because it is a human organic material — costs a lot of gems to stabilise

## Strategic Implications

- The food constraint makes it clear how dependent the town is on the exporter — creates another goal to upgrade
- The player cannot reach the edges of the map until they can become non-reliant on the importer for food
- This happens when they can buy food from the [[Sanctuary/Town/NPC System|townspeople]] (though daily amounts are limited)
- Later in the game, the player will have enough food and the [[Sanctuary/Crystals/Crystal Power & Networks|teleporter network]] to survive mostly comfortably at night

## Late Game: Farming

- Very late in the game, an NPC will offer the player some way to establish a farm (very endgame)
- In the late game, this turns into a farming game
