## Mana System

- The game has a mana system, and the player needs pots to replenish mana
- Mana recovers very slowly over time by itself
- Pots are made from [[Sanctuary/Crystals/Crystal Overview|crystals]]
- Eventually mana requirements become quite large, requiring larger mana pots
- Crystal hierarchy equates to different levels of mana pots
