## Extraction, mob killing,  leveleed duneons

The biologists are are a race of being who prefer to live in the dark. They have travelled the dimensions widely and have mastered the art of generating monsters and creatures from having
sampled all the various alien species spread out through the universe.

They come to this world to tap the power and to gather the minerals. 

They have setup the resource extraction and gathering nodes in the caves in this world as they avoid the sunlight. And they have filled up the caves with their creations to guard they magic resource gathering

The caves do adhere logically to the overall ground structure. however consider if the cliff
is lvl 1, and another cliff lvl1, then we can connect up two cliffs by, int he cave having a 
level -1 <--- this section is then underground.
So then we can have some coherence to the map whilst still having caves large enough
for level variety.

Why is the caves open  ?
To ensure the monsters get fed and to acquire biological substrate the cave entrances work
as a trap

Once you enter the cave, the way behind is blocked.
The player must defeat all the monsters, to unlock the exit mechanism, or get to the exit mechanism somehow and avoid all the monsters. 
The caves do slowly respawn monsters, but this is carefully balances witht the minerals
resource regenraiton rate so the player is alwasy presented with some challenge.


Each can have multiple instances and configurations, which the biologists have the power 
to do. It is a good thing that sunlight defeats their magic and abilities as no doubt they
would otherwise conquer the universe. (In fact this is one of their motivations in seeking out power)

So therefore we can randomise the cave layout. The biologists randomise the cave layout
as a method of confusing any people that enter their caves. And they have levels or 
verticality because they can maximise their power production by verticality. And usually
the deeper that you go the more power and therefore, the bioligists will allocate more 
resources to safeguarding those areas. Once a plyaer has accesed a level they can teleport
to the region because the player has knowledge fo the system and can trick the trip entranche
mechanism.

As for level design we always have some small areas in the cave with limited resources
so the player can atleast grab some minerals then rush to the exit, avoiding most of the
monsters.

The caves have: 
 - miners
 - artifacts from the biologist drilling operations you can steal
 - hask
 - catalst
 - other monster item drops to sell in the real world
