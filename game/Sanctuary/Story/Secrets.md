# Secrets

- These other dimensions **feed off the consciousness** of humans
- What humans take for granted is missing in these other dimensions
- Certain human activities overall decrease consciousness — and this is extracted from Earth's dimensions

> [!warning] Major Story Revelation
> Unknown to humanity, there are also other portals from the other dimensions to **even more dimensions**. Finding this out in-game is a huge revelation.
