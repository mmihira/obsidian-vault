# NPC System

## NPC Advancement

How do NPCs start to advance?
- They build a home
- They build a business
- When NPCs get rich they provide benefits

### Supply Lines

- Secure some supply lines for basics
- Secure some supply lines for advanced materials
- Advanced supply lines
- Entertainment
- Parties and celebrations are hosted for every great milestone the town goes through

NPCs start building out from the central town teleportation square.

## NPC Progression System

Clear sense of progress with **no time limit**.

### Progression Steps

1. Keep the cooking pot filled with food for two days
2. NPCs will be inclined to start gathering materials
3. If cooking pot goes empty, they stop accumulating materials
4. NPCs will actually accumulate materials by themselves as long as the player does the minimum required

### Reaching Self-Sustaining Food Supply

- Requires renewable magic supply
- Some people to go hunting every day

### Progression of Goals

1. Secure food supply
2. Secure some supply lines for basics

## NPC Crystal Patches

- Some NPCs will mine their own crystal patch to produce items for the player
- Early in the game, player is tasked with protecting NPC crystal patches
