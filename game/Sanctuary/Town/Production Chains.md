# Production Chains

## Crystals as Power Sources

- Able to source energies from crystals and accumulate them in large tanks
- Enables production chain to produce pots etc from accumulated magic essences

## Production Buildings

- Production building plans are placed and then built
- They have a menu which the player can interact with
- They have animations for when working

## Automation Alternatives

- Instead of belts, use **magic** to move items
- **Central logistics tower** — enables teleportation from certain buildings to other buildings
- Need some kind of animation to show import/export of items

## Customisation / Vanity

- Decorate the town with little items to spruce it up

## What Does the Player Build and Sell?

- **Magic wands** — from cutting down trees
