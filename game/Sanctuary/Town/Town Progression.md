# Town Progression

## Building Gameplay

1. Player selects building from menu
2. Player places it on the map
3. Further requirement: relevant NPCs are available for cut scenes

## Tech Progression

- NPC Shop + Gem field
- Have to wait until NPC gem fields grow to start buying potions

## Growing Town From Nothing to Something

- Building and upgrading buildings
- Building certain buildings unlocks more things
- Player can decorate the town

### Town Renown System

- Player must upgrade the town to make it more attractive to potential settlers

## Resource Management

- Building towers will require certain resources
- Building up the town is one option where the town starts small and exploration progress unlocks things
- Possible milestone event: a fight with many allies against monsters that come through
