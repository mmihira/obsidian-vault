# Backstory

You begin your journey in the overworld, living with your parents in your hometown. After studying crystal growing and saving up enough gems, you finally have the means to transport yourself — and all your belongings — to the other dimension where you hope to make it big as a gem exporter.

However, during your journey through the portal, an irregularity occurs, and you lose most of your belongings...
