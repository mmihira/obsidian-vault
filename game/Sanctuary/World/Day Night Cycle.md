# Day Night Cycle

## Night Effects

- [[Sanctuary/Crystals/Crystal Types & Structures#Dark Crystals|Dark crystals]] activate at night which spawn stronger enemies that will attack the player
- Nighttime enforces a reset and checkpoint

## Sleep Mechanics

- Sleep always ends at **6AM**
- Less than 6 hours sleep causes penalties
- Sleeping with a full stomach gives benefits
- Being famished prevents recovery and XP gain

## Design Considerations

- Night forces a reset and a mandatory checkpoint the player must go through
- However enforcing it is also annoying — need some kind of notification system to signal to the player of optional but beneficial events
- Player cannot control how much they sleep — if player goes to sleep they always wake up at 6AM
