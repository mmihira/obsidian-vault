# Factions

Different factions have developed on Earth:

## Expansionists

Advocate for massively expanding humanity into alternative dimensions and importing resources back to earth for consumption.

## Death Cults

Believe alternative dimensions are the lure of the devil. They want to destroy the links to other dimensions and are incredibly violent.

- There was an unfortunate incident where unexpectedly a link was destroyed, trapping all expeditioners on the other side who are presumed now to be dead.
- The **expansionists and death cults are at war** with each other.

## Research Organisations

Seek to understand the other dimensions further before deciding on further actions.

- Have strict dogmatic beliefs about how the universe functions and other dimensions function
- Believe researching other dimensions will help to understand the universe more and develop science to a point enough to **destroy magic**
- Scientific progress has been slowed down in their view due to magic

## Hidden Factions

- **Dimensional civilisations** who migrate from one source of spirit to others and seek to harvest the spirit for their own use — to live longer and make civilisations subservient to themselves
- A **Tom Bombadil type character** — wise, has travelled between dimensions using his own devices, seeks to maintain order between dimensions. Origins and masters are mysterious. One of the main "guides" in the story line.

> [!info] Unknown to humanity, there are also other portals from the other dimensions to even more dimensions. Finding this out in-game is a **huge revelation**.
